Soccer

Soccer, otherwise known as Futbol (football) is a sport where players work as a team, kicking a ball around, to try to score a goal on the opponent's goalie.
While playing soccer, players will be able to:

  • Learn teamwork
  • Work on communication
  • Identify their strengths

More Details

A soccer team is comprised of 11 players, one of them being the goalkeeper.
The positions include:

  • Goalkeeper
  • Defense
  • Midfielder
  • Forward

Each player will have the choice of the position they would like to play. If the player does not have a desired position, the manager will assign them a position.
